About me

Hi! I'm Leonardo Glorioso, also known online as Crllect. I'm a passionate computer science student based in Cambridge, MA. I'm a Front-end Web Developer learning Full-stack, currently focusing on trying to branch out as much as possible, and diversify my portfolio.

Most of my projects have been Web Proxies, blending aesthetic frontends with elegant and fast backends. My prefered languages, tools & frameworks include Astro, TypeScript, Tailwind, Remix, Next.js, SCSS and Bun, though I mostly stick to a Astro, Tailwind and Bun stack.

I'm tryng improve my full-stack and DevOps skills, so every chance I get im building something new, unique and fun. I love to create websites, and integrating web interfaces with physical components.
